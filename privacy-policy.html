Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024 | Effective: At launch

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Brain Teaser ("we," "us," "our," or "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and your family's information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our web application at thebrainteaser.com and associated services.

Please read this policy carefully. By using The Brain Teaser, you acknowledge that you have read and understand this policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

2. Compliance & Legal Framework

This App operates under the following data protection laws:

  • COPPA (US): Children's Online Privacy Protection Act — for users under 13 in the US
  • NDPA (Nigeria): Nigeria Data Protection Regulation — for all users in Nigeria
  • GDPR (EU): General Data Protection Regulation — for users in the EU
  • UK GDPR: UK General Data Protection Regulation

We design this App to be privacy-first, locally-stored, and ad-free for children. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

The App does not require sign-up or login. However, you may optionally provide:

  • Name: Used to personalize your profile and progress tracking (stored only on your device)
  • Grade level or profession interest: To tailor content difficulty and recommendations
  • Contact email (if you write to us): For support, feedback, or questions

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and browser info: Type, OS, browser, screen size (for responsive design only)
  • Usage analytics: Which modes you play, how long you spend, which questions you attempt, and your scores — collected only if you opt in to analytics (see §5)
  • Cookies and storage: We use browser localStorage to save your progress, settings, and achievements on your device. This is not shared with us unless you explicitly sync to an account (future feature).

3.3 No Audio or Biometric Collection

The App uses your browser's built-in text-to-speech (browser speech synthesis API) for reading-aloud games. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored. Speech synthesis runs entirely on your device.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use data only for these purposes:

  • Core function: Saving your progress, scores, levels, and unlocked content on your device
  • Personalization: Adapting difficulty, recommending subjects, and showing relevant achievements
  • Improvement: Identifying broken features, crashes, or UX issues (with your consent)
  • Support: Responding to your questions or feedback
  • Legal compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations or protecting safety

We do NOT:

  • Show targeted ads based on behavior
  • Share profile data with third parties
  • Use behavioral profiling or tracking pixels
  • Sell or license personal information

5. Analytics & Optional Tracking

The App may use privacy-respecting analytics (e.g., Plausible, Fathom) to measure page views, session duration, and which features are most used.

  • No personal identification: Analytics are aggregated and anonymized
  • No cookies or tracking: We do not set cookies for tracking; analytics run serverless
  • COPPA compliant: For children under 13, analytics do not fingerprint or correlate users
  • Opt-in: You can disable analytics in Settings (when implemented)
Children under 13 (COPPA): If a child is using the App, we recommend that a parent or guardian review this policy and consent to analytics collection. A parent may request deletion of any analytics data by contacting us (see §11).

6. Affiliate Links & Sponsored Content

The App includes a "Brain Shelf" feature that recommends books, learning products, and courses relevant to each topic (e.g., "good books for logic puzzles"). These links are affiliate links to retailers like Amazon Associates, Jumia, Udemy, and Coursera.

  • Disclosure: All affiliate links are marked with "Sponsored" or "Partner Link" and carry rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" tags
  • What it means: If you click a link and buy, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you
  • Data: Clicking a link takes you to the retailer's site. Their privacy policy then applies. We do not receive your purchase data.
  • Children: On the children's side, affiliate recommendations are framed for parents ("Books to help your child grow"), not pitched directly to kids

7. Third-Party Services

The App relies on these third-party services:

  • Hosting & deployment: Vercel or Netlify (static file delivery). Vercel Privacy | Netlify Privacy
  • Fonts: Google Fonts (downloaded locally, no tracking). Google Privacy
  • Analytics (if enabled): Plausible or Fathom (GDPR-compliant, no tracking cookies). Plausible Privacy
  • Affiliate programs: Amazon, Jumia, Udemy, etc. Their terms apply when you visit their sites.

We have no control over third-party privacy practices. We choose vendors carefully and favor those with privacy-first commitments. If you have concerns, contact us.

8. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Device storage (localStorage): Remains on your device until you clear browser cache/cookies, or until the App is deleted
  • Support emails: Kept for 2 years unless you request deletion sooner
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained for 12 months; deleted after that
  • Crash reports (if enabled): Kept for 30 days then deleted

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

For all users:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data (e.g., support emails, analytics records)
  • Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate information

For users in the EU / UK (GDPR):

  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Opt out of analytics or future communications
  • Restriction: Restrict how we process your data

For users in Nigeria (NDPA):

  • Right to be forgotten: Request deletion of personal data
  • Data transparency: Know how we use your information
  • Remedies: File a complaint with the NDPC if you believe we've violated your rights

For parents (COPPA):

If your child has used the App without your knowledge or consent, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of all data collected from your child
  • Prevent further collection of information from your child
  • Review any information we have about your child

To exercise any of these rights, contact us (see §11).

10. Security

Your device-stored progress is protected by your device's security. Since The Brain Teaser is a client-side app (no backend account system at launch), all progress is encrypted by your browser's localStorage mechanism and remains on your device.

We take the following security measures:

  • HTTPS only: All communication with our servers is encrypted (TLS/SSL)
  • No passwords or sensitive data required: The App does not ask for passwords or payment info
  • Secure hosting: Deployment via Vercel/Netlify with DDoS and intrusion protection
  • Regular reviews: We audit for security and privacy risks regularly
